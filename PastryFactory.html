Pitch

Combine delicious pastries in Pastry Factory to create mechanisms and repair cake machines. You are the new chief repairer of the wonderful world of pastries.

Solve the different puzzles that are available, use donuts, profiteroles and other pastries, avoid slippery frosting and blocking biscuits, and finally join the centerpieces of the machine.
