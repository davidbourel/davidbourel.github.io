005 : The Operator

Gameplay programmer / Level Designer / Sound Designer

The Operator was created during 1 intensive week. It was made by a 6 student team, for the year 2017-2018. The player controls the operator of a small squad of soldiers during the First World War. He must manage the difficulties and morale of his squad, through mail management, in order to survive the conflict. By deciding to send mails as they are, censure them or throw them out, the player impacts his resources (morale, fodd, military force) and the narrative.

This project was really interesting for me, as it was the first time I had to work on a game with a strong narrative. I worked on the gameplay (grab and discard letters), the ambiance and the music.
