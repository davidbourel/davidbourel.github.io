003 : Slum77

Gameplay and AI programmer / Level Designer / Cinematic Designer

Slum77 is a 2-player horror game, where players embody prisoners in an underwater station invaded by deadly creatures. Without any means of defense and no way to communicate with each other, they will have to survive and find a way out of the station, by helping each other or not.

The biggest challenges we have design-wise on this game are linked to our intentions. We want to make a multiplayer horror game that is not forcing the players to cooperate, neither to be in confrontation. An AI is ruling the station, and we want the players to make their own choices: Obei the AI's orders and stay separated, or disobey the orders and cooperate to find another way to leave the station. This implies 2 things. First, we have to plan everything the players could do, because we want to give them freedom of action. Every edgecase has to be considered and designed, so that the experience works, even though it is not the same for both players. Then, as we want the players to make a choice between obedience and disobedience, we have to ensure that they clearly understand the choice, and that the AI doesn't represent an absolute game system that is giving the objectives, that it can be bypassed.
