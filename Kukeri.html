Kukeri

Job

Gameplay and AI programmer.

Pitch:

Kukeri is a game of cooperation. They must be alert to the dangers and the Melalo, a gypsy demon who chases them. They must cooperate to release a totem in the center of a circle while defending the Melalo to survive until dawn. Players must relay to push the totem, reactivate it and capture the monster's attention. The camera is fixed, with a view from top down.

The players each control his character who moves by trotting. The player uses the left stick to move and only the button A to interact with the different elements.
