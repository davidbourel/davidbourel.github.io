20.000 Lieues sans la mer

Job

Game Designer & AI Programmer.

Pitch

20 000 Lieux Sans La Mer is a 2D platform / reflection game where the character has the ability to absorb and recruit liquids. The challenges presented to the player relate to fluid physics and the interactions between water and landscape. Furthermore, the size, weight and ability to jump are influenced by the amount of water you absorb, forcing the player to think and order his actions to solve the different models proposed.
